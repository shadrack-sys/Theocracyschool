About The School

Theocracy Complex School, Kumasi Campus, was established on 6th September, 2015. The school has its main campus at Homedakrom which is nearer to Otiakrom in the Akwapem South District in Eastern Region of Ghana. The proprietor of the school is Apostle Kadmiel E.H. Agbalenyoh who is also the Missionary in charge of Seventh Day Theocratic World Congregation.
Motto: Becoming like God in character.
VISION STATEMENT
To engage learners to portray God's character and equips them to reach their unique potentials spiritually,physically, socially and academically.
MISSION STATEMENT
The school seeks to be a learning organization that produces life-long learners and responsible citizens that will meet the current socio-economic challenges.
CORE VALUES
Humility, Creativity and Excellence
